Stroke Types

  • Ischemic Stroke – Caused by blocked arteries.
  • Hemorrhagic Stroke – Caused by bleeding in the brain.
  • Transient Ischemic Attack (TIA) – A temporary blockage, often called a mini-stroke.

Treatment Approaches

  • Medications – Blood thinners, clot busters, and antihypertensives.
  • Surgical Procedures – Stent placement, clot removal, and aneurysm repair.
  • Rehabilitation – Physical, speech, and occupational therapy.

Monitoring & Prevention

  • Lifestyle Changes – Healthy diet, exercise, and quitting smoking.
  • Regular Check-Ups – Routine health monitoring to prevent risks.
  • AI-Powered Risk Assessment – Early detection and personalized recommendations.
